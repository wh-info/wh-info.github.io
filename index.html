sde.whtype.info

Use this tool to identify your wormhole connection by type.

whtype.info tool is the result of a continuous scanning project focusing on Anoikis space in EVE Online.

Check attributes of any wormhole type, or select different attributes as filters to track down desired wormhole connection.

Initial data was pulled from Anoik.is, EVE Ref, UniWiki and several other sources including private research database.

All information is crosschecked, updated and verified by constant scanning and on-grid observations.
